ABOUT ETHERSCXPE

Etherscxpe began as a solo journey—Bonolo Solomon, a musician with a passion for visual storytelling, picked up a camera in 2018 to craft professional images for his music brand. What started as a necessity soon became an obsession. Through relentless practice and an artist’s eye, Bonolo’s photography evolved, capturing not just images but emotions and narratives.

Word spread. Clients—drawn to his unique ability to freeze moments with authenticity—began asking for more. From intimate portraits to vibrant event coverage, each project reinforced a truth: a picture doesn’t just speak; it tells a thousand words.

Today, Etherscxpe is a testament to that philosophy. We’ve expanded beyond photography into graphic and web design, but our core remains unchanged—transforming ideas into visual stories that resonate. Whether through a lens, a logo, or a website, we’re here to make your vision seen, felt, and remembered.
